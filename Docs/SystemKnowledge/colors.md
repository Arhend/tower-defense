# The Highwayman Palette (Complete)

A 20-color pixel art palette inspired by highwayman paintings - deep shadows, warm candlelight, romantic 18th-century atmosphere, with water, skin tones, and greenery.

## Aesthetic Notes

This palette captures the theatrical, dramatic quality of highwayman paintings with vibrant jewel tones, strong chiaroscuro contrast, and rich saturation. Perfect for moonlit robberies, forest hideaways, candlelit taverns, and romantic outlaw scenes. The dual water and skin tones allow for more dimensional rendering of key elements.
